Transmitter:
	Measures true zero.
	Measures low and slow.
	Measures high and fast.
	Displays CPM and uSv/h.
	Transmits CPM to the receiver where the CPM can be observed remotely.


Receiver:
	Receives CPM from transmitter.
	Displays CPM and uSv/h.
	Receives counts as their happening, from transmitter.
	Displays each count on an LED and plays a click for each count on its own speaker.
